My Life Story

I was born in a trashcan near a local hospital. My parents left me immediately, having not enough supplies to sustain all. I lived under a bridge that leaked constantly. I moved to California by foot, only to get severely injured in an earthquake. I was kept in a very unkempt hospital, where moss grew on the side of the bed I rested on. I was then thrown out, due to the hospital closing for reconstruction. I dragged myself all the way back to where I came from, and things attacked all around. But when all was still in the world, I still got my High School Diploma.
